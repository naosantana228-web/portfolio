Project framing

Simple, useful, and connected to the work.

Context

Regional teams needed a repeatable starting point for compliance learning. Recreating the visual layout and course structure for each implementation would have repeated setup and made consistency harder to maintain.

My contribution

I created the complete visual layout and placeholder structure in Absorb LMS, then shared the reusable template across teams so each team could populate content without rebuilding the design and delivery structure. I also supported the wider 15 course programme through curriculum organisation, development, review, assessment, feedback, and LMS delivery.

Key design decisions

  • Established the page layout, visual rhythm, and placeholder positions before regional content population.
  • Separated the reusable shell from adaptable content so teams could preserve the design while updating what they needed.
  • Used one shared template as the starting point for regional adoption instead of local rebuilds.

Evidence and transparency

The template and regional reuse are described from my professional account. The three interface images preserve supplied Absorb layouts and replace only visible sample content; they are contextual examples, not untouched production screenshots or proof of a specific live tenant. The 15 course scope and LMS delivery derive from the original public portfolio case. No numerical efficiency or adoption claim is made.
