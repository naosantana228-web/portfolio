Project 01 · AI adoption

Reviewer Onboarding: AI Assist

A fictional onboarding journey that helps reviewers adopt an AI supported workflow with clear expectations, guided practice, decision support, and human accountability.

  • SolutionOnboarding journey and AI decision support
  • RoleLearning journey, scenario practice, decision support
  • ToolsBrowser learning prototype
  • EvidencePublic source sample

Public design evidence

The journey, practice, and support when decisions happen.

Project framing

Simple, useful, and connected to the work.

Context

Reviewers need to adopt an AI supported workflow without losing judgement, accountability, or confidence. A fictional onboarding journey makes the change concrete, keeps safeguards visible, and creates a safe place to practise decisions.

My contribution

I structured the journey, expectation setting, guided practice, decision logic, feedback, and support at the moment of need. I then translated responsible use principles into clear actions, escalation choices, and safeguards for human review.

Key design decisions

  • Introduced the workflow through a journey centred on the reviewer role rather than a feature tour.
  • Placed realistic decisions and feedback before independent use so judgement remains central.
  • Connected onboarding to a concise decision framework that stays available during work.

Evidence and transparency

The three public images demonstrate the fictional learning journey, guided module, practice structure, and decision support. This fully shareable concept does not claim production adoption, learner results, or business impact.

Learning ecosystem

How this solution connects.

The deliverable is one part of a performance system. The surrounding analysis, support, and evidence make it useful beyond a single learning moment.

Before

Set expectations

Clarify why the workflow is changing, where AI can help, where human judgement remains essential, and how to escalate uncertainty.

During

Practise the decisions

Use guided workflow steps, examples, scenarios, feedback, and safeguards to build confident and accountable use.

After

Support responsible use

Keep the decision framework available during work and use questions, quality signals, and review findings to improve support.
